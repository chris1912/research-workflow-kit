# Research Methods

Codex annotation: Public entrypoint prepared by Codex on 2026-07-15.

This directory contains a bundled methods skill whose detailed reference material is retained under `ars/`. The bundled material remains third-party content; see `docs/THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md` and `docs/licenses/academic-research-skills-codex-license.txt` before redistribution or commercial use.

Read `docs/START_HERE.html` first. Use this skill for question refinement, systematic-review or meta-analysis planning, peer-review simulation, editorial decisions, citation and integrity checks, experiment planning, reproducibility validation, and statistical-interpretation planning.

## Working Rules

- Separate protocol decisions, extracted evidence, interpretation, and writing.
- Record inclusion criteria, search dates, exclusions, and unresolved uncertainty.
- Do not invent data, citations, reviewer identities, or statistical results.
- Require human confirmation for study design, final claims, and submission-ready text.

Use the neutral adapters in `src/workflow_lab/adapters/` for discovery, parsing, corpus QA, literature mapping, and synthesis. Include `docs/START_HERE.html` in phase summaries.
