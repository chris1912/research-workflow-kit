# Chinese Academic Style Guide

## General Principles

Chinese academic writing should be formal, concise, logically explicit, objective, terminology-consistent, and evidence-bounded.

## Prefer

- "实验结果表明..."
- "该方法在...方面表现出较好的性能"
- "该设计有助于缓解..."
- "在所评估的数据集上..."
- "仍需进一步验证..."
- "与现有方法相比，该方法主要关注..."

## Avoid

- "这个方法很好"
- "效果特别明显"
- "非常厉害"
- "完美解决"
- "彻底消除"
- "显著优于所有方法"
- "具有重大意义" without explanation

## Logical Connectors

Use transitions to make reasoning explicit:

- 因此
- 进一步地
- 与此同时
- 然而
- 为解决上述问题
- 基于此
- 具体而言
- 实验结果表明

## Sentence-Level Rules

- Prefer clear subject-predicate-object structures.
- Avoid excessively long sentences.
- Place conditions and limitations near the claim.
- Keep technical terms consistent.
- Replace vague words such as "很多", "较大", and "明显" with evidence or cautious phrasing.
