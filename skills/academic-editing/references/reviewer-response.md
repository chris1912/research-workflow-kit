# Reviewer Response Workflow

## Tone

Reviewer responses must be polite, specific, non-defensive, evidence-based, and clear about what changed.

## Standard Structure

1. Thank the reviewer.
2. Restate the concern briefly.
3. Agree, partially agree, or respectfully clarify.
4. Explain what was changed or what change is proposed.
5. Point to manuscript location if available.
6. Provide revised text if useful.

## English Template

```text
Reviewer Comment:
...

Response:
Thank you for your insightful comment. We agree that ... To address this issue, we have revised Section X to clarify ... Specifically, we added ...

Revision:
We have revised Section X, Page X, Lines X-X as follows:
"..."
```

## Chinese Template

```text
审稿人意见：
...

回复：
感谢审稿人的建设性意见。我们认同该问题对于……的重要性。针对该意见，我们已在修订稿中……

修改位置：
第 X 节，第 X 页，第 X-X 行。

修改后文本：
"..."
```

## Handling Disagreement

When the authors disagree:

- acknowledge the reviewer's perspective;
- explain the reason with evidence;
- avoid emotional or dismissive wording;
- offer a clarifying revision when possible;
- do not imply that unsupported experiments or analyses were added.
