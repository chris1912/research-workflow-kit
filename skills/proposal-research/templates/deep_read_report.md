## Evidence boundary / 证据边界

Separate every note into one of:

- **extract**: words tightly bound to the source
- **claim**: source-supported assertion usable after check
- **inference**: interpretation that goes beyond the extract
- **uncertainty**: missing methods detail, conflict, or weak support

Do not invent page numbers, statistics, or conclusions that are not readable in the available full text.
