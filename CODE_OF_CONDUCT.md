# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Expectations / 期望

**English.** Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and
attentive to scientific integrity when discussing issues, pull requests, and
workflow design.

**中文。** 参与者在议题、拉取请求与工作流设计讨论中应保持尊重、建设性，并重视科学诚信。

## Unacceptable behavior / 不可接受行为

- Harassment or personal attacks / 骚扰或人身攻击
- Fabricated evidence or fabricated citations / 捏造证据或捏造引文
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of third-party work / 剽窃或歪曲第三方工作
- Sharing credentials, private manuscripts, personal or health data, or
  unpublished results in public channels / 在公开渠道分享凭据、未发表手稿、个人或健康数据或未公开结果
- Instructing or requesting paywall bypass or other unlawful access / 指示或请求付费墙绕过或其他非法获取

## Scope / 适用范围

This policy applies to project spaces maintained for
`chris1912/research-workflow-kit`, including issues, pull requests, and
discussions.

本准则适用于 `chris1912/research-workflow-kit` 的项目空间，包括议题、拉取请求与讨论。

## Reporting / 报告

For conduct concerns that are not security vulnerabilities, open a private
maintainer contact if available, or use a non-public channel preferred by the
maintainers. For credential exposure or security issues, follow
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).

非安全类行为问题请通过维护者可用的私下渠道联系。凭据泄露或安全问题请遵循
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md)。
